About The Founder

APOSTLE N N MCOTELI

  • Apostle Mcoteli was called by the Lord Jesus Christ as a successful business man and an award winning insurance broker, to fulltime ministry. He was at that time serving under the Full Gospel Church of God in Southern Africa. Together with his wife Tandi, they founded their ministry in an informal settlement in East London in 1998.

  • Apostle Mcoteli became the first African preacher to preach via television in the Eastern Cape through the then TBN Eastern Cape. God has gifted him in raising leaders. His apostolic ministry quickly spread throughout the nation of South Africa.

  • To date he has ordained 25 Pastors including Bishops, and 24 Elders. These are responsible for the work in the 8 provinces which have a total of 85 churches spread across.

  • He is also the provincial chairperson of The Evangelical Alliance of South Africa (TEASA), Eastern Cape, a body that gathers together numerous ministry leaders across the body of Christ. He is currently having a weekly radio program called ‘The Word of God is The Bread of Life’ with Link FM. By God’s grace, together with his wife, they founded two fully fledged Christian Schools, Faith Revival Christian School and Bizana Christian Academy, which start from Grade R to 12.

  • He is a Board Member of the African Education Enterprise (AEE), a body that oversees Christian Education in Africa and Scandinavia countries. He himself is a theologian having obtained Honors in Theology from University of Fort Hare. He is currently pursuing a Masters degree in Theology with South African Theological Seminary. He is also concurrently studying towards Masters degree in rural development with the University of Fort Hare.

  • He is family man blessed with 4 children a daughter and 3 sons. Together with his wife Tandi, they also serve as Marriage Counsellors, a ministry that has reaches multitude of families, even beyond their church walls. Apostle Mcoteli is a man of faith, a visionary and a pioneer. He has travelled and ministered both in Africa and USA. He is sold out for the gospel of Jesus Christ and his passion is evident in his work with the poorest of his people. God tested them to the point where they lost all the material possessions they had acquired.
