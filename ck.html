Covenant Keepers is the department that seeks to develop men in our society.

Vision:
“Develop the whole man.” 2 Timothy 3:17

Our Mission:
• Develop soul, boy and spirit
• Pursuit of spiritual excellence
• Empower men by preaching and teaching the word of God
• Corporate Excellence

Strategic Goals:
• Advocating for responsible and proud fathers.
• Ecalcating positive habbits and attitude in our society.
• Win more men for Christ.
• Enabling the voice of integrity to be heard in the community, publicly condemn misbehavior.
• Developing skills.
